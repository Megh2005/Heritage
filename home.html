OUR MISSION AND VISION IS

Developing skilled and highly competent Civil Engineers to excel in Education, Research and Entrepreneurship, so that the department will be recognized high in a global scenario.

OUR MAIN TARGET IS TO

Empower the students with broad and in-depth knowledge in Civil Engineering fundamentals and their applications in practical as well as professional fields to meet socio-economic challenges. As well as we educate the students in the latest technologies in Civil Engineering, imbibe in them human values, self-confidence, team work and independent thinking in solving diverse problems in the related field so that they can serve the society.

OUR PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

The graduate students in Civil Engineering from Heritage Institute of Technology, are expected to Establish themselves as successful Civil Engineering professionals and entrepreneurs also to prove their academic and research expertise in the field of higher studies through Masters and/or Doctoral programme at different institutes in India and abroad as well as to demonstrate their sense of professionalism, leadership qualities and team work abilities in their professions.

What Is
K-onstructz ?

DEPARTMENTAL MAGAZINE

K-onstructz is the official departmental magazine of the department of Civil Engineering of Heritage Insstitute of Technology. It gets published in online mode. All the faculty members and students show a great interest in the publication of this magazine

STAGE TO SHOW CREATIVITY

K-onstructz is the stage for everyone to show their own creativity and talents throgh their pens and lens. Folks can contribute their writings and cliks to enrich the magazine. students who are good editors also show their skills by joining the editorial team

HISTORY OF K-ONSTRUCTZ

K-onstructz was first published in 2019. It was the month of April when the chariot of winning had taken a kickstart. From then every year the department of Civil Engineering @ HITK is publishing K-onstructz. We are assuring that this good trend will be continued in future too
